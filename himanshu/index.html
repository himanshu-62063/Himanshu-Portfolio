My Projects

Soul Heal- Stress Relief Website

Soulheal is a healthcare website designed to help users manage stress through various therapies and tools. It offers audio therapy (music and podcasts via Spotify), video therapy, child therapy, reading therapy (motivational books via Amazon), laughing therapy, yoga therapy, and spiritual therapy. Additionally, it features a chatbot for personalized therapy and a consult a doctor option, all aimed at improving mental well-being.

Prescripto

Prescripto is an online doctor appointment platform that simplifies booking and managing medical consultations. It features secure user registration, appointment booking, payment integration, an admin panel, doctor ratings, and upcoming AI-powered doctor recommendations based on specific medical needs.

one district one product

One District One Product (ODOP) is an initiative by the Indian government to promote and support the production of unique products or crafts that are specific to each district. The goal is to boost the local economy, create employment opportunities, and preserve traditional crafts by developing these products on a larger scale. Each district identifies a product or craft with a strong local identity, and the government provides support for marketing, branding, and export, helping local artisans and manufacturers reach a wider market.
